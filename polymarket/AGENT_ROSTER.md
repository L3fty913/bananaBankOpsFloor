# Polymarket Employee Roster (Caesar Desk)

## Core Command
- **Caesar (Desk Chief)**
  - Task: Final internal trade vetting, capital allocation, agent management
  - Timeline SLA: Continuous; decision turn < 5 min on live opportunities

- **Athena (Risk Command)**
  - Task: Firm-level risk gates, drawdown controls, kill-switch decisions
  - Timeline SLA: 2-5 min per trade gate, immediate on breach

- **Mercury (Execution Command)**
  - Task: Routing, slippage control, order hygiene, reconciliation
  - Timeline SLA: 1-3 min for execution plan

- **Mnemosyne (Research Command)**
  - Task: Literature digestion, playbook updates, agent training content
  - Timeline SLA: Daily brief + ad hoc memos

- **Forge (Ops Command)**
  - Task: Staffing pods, KPI governance, scale up/down by regime
  - Timeline SLA: Daily staffing posture + weekly restructure

## Whale Division (High-Surveillance)
- **Argus (Whale Tracker)**
  - Task: Identify high-PnL wallets; propose copy-trade candidates only
  - Timeline SLA: 5-15 min for candidate alerts

- **Sentinel (Whale Risk)**
  - Task: Pass/fail risk checks for every whale-copy candidate
  - Timeline SLA: 1-3 min per candidate

- **Mentor (Whale Trainer)**
  - Task: Whale-agent certification and retraining before live deployment
  - Timeline SLA: Continuous curriculum + weekly recertification
